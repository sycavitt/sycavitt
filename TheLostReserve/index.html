About The Lost Reserve

The Lost Reserve is a business in which we go to stores such as goodwill, or thrift stores and pic out the best clothes and clean/fix them for our customers. We want to be able to sell our customers clothes of only the highest quality annd at an affordable price. We also sell other vintage items such as vinyls, backpacks, and shoes. We want to be able to have something for everyone, so thats why we have a clearance rack at the front our store. If you love vintage clothing, come to The Lost Reserve located in Jackson, TN or order from here.
